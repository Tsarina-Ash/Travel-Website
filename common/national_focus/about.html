About Us
We are a travel research website, combining vast quantities of information to bring the user the best offers on flights, hotels and transportation and the service is completely free. The vision of our founder was that travel should be accessible to everyone and that they should have the best and most convenient travel experience possible in the shape of the internet due to the fast pace of the modern world.
The Company was founded in 1977 as a travel agency in Michigan but quickly expanded to the western United States in the 80’s with the Californian boom but in the early 2000’s the company underwent a new change in direction with the popularity of the internet growing, the company went through years of turmoil and devastation before it came out as the product it is today, catering to the user finding the best experience for them as possible.
