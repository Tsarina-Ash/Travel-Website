Visit Vaasa
The Ostrobothnia Museum was founded in 1895 15 minutes away from the city’s center, as the museum of Vaasa it not only surrounds visitors with history of the city but also with science and conducts 3-5 special exhibitions annually.
Tropicalandia is a water park that is fun for a group of people to enjoy with heated water year-round and many rides and slides to hop on and enjoy while drinking some refreshments.

The Original Sokos Hotel is a top of the line place to stay while in Helsinki, offering some of the best customer service at the best price per bargain night ratio, the staff will treat you like family and make sure that you are at home while in their presence.
The Forenom Aparthotel Hotel is an easily recognizable and well known brand for its consistency with its spacious rooms, excellent service and wonderful to be around staff who are always handy.
