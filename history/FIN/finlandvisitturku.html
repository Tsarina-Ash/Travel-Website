Visit Turku
Moominworld is a place to enjoy with the family during the summer months, especially when with children who have grown up watching moominworld on their television and dream of visiting a park with their favorite characters from the show.
The Sibelius Museum is a museum like no other, it is not about history of a civilization, nation or person but rather a shared experience of music and how we all perceive it, thus showing how it has grown through time constantly doing new exhibitions.
The Radisson Blu Marina Palace Hotel is a top of the line place to stay while in Helsinki, offering some of the best customer service at the best price per bargain night ratio, the staff will treat you like family and make sure that you are at home while in their presence.
The Scandic Hamburger Bors Hotel is an easily recognizable and well known brand for its consistency with its spacious rooms, excellent service and wonderful to be around staff who are always handy.
